# Software Factory: Business Model & Revenue Projections

## Executive Summary

Software Factory is an open-source, local-first AI-native SDLC desktop app. The business model follows the **Obsidian/Postman playbook**: free core product drives adoption, revenue comes from team features, cloud services, and marketplace.

**Projected Year 3 ARR: $4.8M - $12M**
**Projected Year 5 ARR: $18M - $45M**

## TIER BREAKDOWN

### 🆓 FREE TIER (Growth Engine)
**Price: $0: forever, no credit card**

What's included:
- Full desktop app (download and run)
- All 5 modules (Requirements, Blueprints, Work Orders, Tests, Feedback)
- Knowledge graph + audit trail
- Ollama integration (unlimited local models)
- MiMo API integration (free cloud models)
- PDF/Markdown export
- 3 projects, 50 requirements per project
- Community templates

**Why free?** This is the growth engine. Every developer who uses it is a potential team lead who pushes for Team tier. Obsidian proved this model: 10M+ users, millions in revenue.

---

### ⚡ PRO TIER (Individual Power Users)
**Price: $12/month or $99/year (save 31%)**

Everything in Free, plus:
- Unlimited projects and requirements
- Custom AI agents (plugin system)
- Advanced export (Notion, Confluence, Jira sync)
- Git integration (auto-commit work orders)
- Priority model access (faster MiMo inference)
- AI-powered code review integration
- Keyboard shortcuts + command palette themes
- Early access to new features

**Target:** Solo developers, freelancers, indie hackers
**Conversion rate from Free:** 5-8%

---

### 👥 TEAM TIER (Primary Revenue Driver)
**Price: $29/user/month or $249/user/year**

Everything in Pro, plus:
- **Real-time collaboration** (multiple users on same project)
- **Cloud sync** (encrypted project backup)
- **Team workspace** (shared requirements, blueprints)
- **Role-based access** (admin, editor, viewer)
- **Comment and review system** (inline on requirements/blueprints)
- **Shared knowledge graph** (team-wide institutional knowledge)
- **Team templates** (private template library)
- **Slack/Discord integration** (notifications)
- **API access** (CI/CD integration)

**Target:** Teams of 3-50 developers
**Conversion rate from Free:** 2-4%
**This is the main revenue driver**: team seats compound

---

### 🏢 ENTERPRISE TIER (High-Value Contracts)
**Price: $49/user/month (annual contracts, minimum 20 seats)**

Everything in Team, plus:
- **SSO/SAML** (Okta, Azure AD, Google Workspace)
- **SOC 2 compliance** (audit logs, data retention policies)
- **Self-hosted option** (run on company infrastructure)
- **Custom model hosting** (bring your own GPU cluster)
- **Dedicated support** (4-hour SLA)
- **Onboarding and training** (dedicated CSM for 50+ seats)
- **Custom integrations** (ServiceNow, SAP, Salesforce)
- **White-label option** (rebrand for internal tools)
- **Advanced analytics** (team productivity metrics, AI usage dashboards)
- **Data residency** (choose where data lives)

**Target:** Companies with 50-10,000+ developers
**Deal size:** $50K - $500K/year ACV
**Sales cycle:** 2-6 months
